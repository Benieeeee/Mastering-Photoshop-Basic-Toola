SHAPE TOOL


The eraser tool is primarily used to change the colors of the pixels to either transparent or to match the color of the file’s background. It is usually located on the left-hand toolbar and also activated through a shortcut by pressing the “E” on your keyboard. The eraser tool can be modified according to the user’s preferences, functioning similarly to the brush tool. The user is able to change features such as its hardness and size in the options bar. The tool is used by selecting a layer and pressing the tool to the desired area that you want to erase on your canvas once it is activated. Its icon is symbolized as an eraser on the toolbar.
